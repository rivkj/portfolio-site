Cultivé

As a Social Media and Marketing Intern at Cultivé Digital Marketing Agency, I supported branding and digital campaigns for clients including Nautiste, Nourish Skin and Wellness, and Skin Care by Kelly. Responsibilities included designing Instagram Stories, developing graphics and captions, assisting with content strategy, and maintaining consistency with brand identity. I collaborated with the design team to produce visual assets and stayed up to date on social media trends to ensure relevant and effective content.

Forme

This project creatively reimagines Forme Studio, a conceptual pottery studio, blending branding and design to capture the essence of its rich atmosphere. Through a carefully crafted minimalist logo and earthy color palette, the goal was to embody the warmth, artistry, and timeless charm of a place where craft and creativity meet.

Lumière Luxe

Lumière Luxe & Co. /loo·mee·air/ is a concept for a sustainable e-commerce platform offering products that prioritize personal well-being and environmental responsibility. The brand’s name, derived from the French word for “light,” reflects its mission to bring ethical, often-overlooked products into the spotlight—promoting conscious consumerism over fast fashion and exploitation.
